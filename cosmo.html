COSMO MELODY

Swaragini

From Ragas to various types of thatas, Indian Classical Music carries the magic to spellbound millions of people. It’s a hidden treasure with benefits such as improving concentration, calming minds, stabilizing health, and many other strong effects on mental and physical health. Swaragini aims at attracting people towards rich Indian Classical Music and making them take a step towards learning it. We especially want youngsters to take part in it enthusiastically. Indian Classical Music depicts the rich musical heritage of India from the education (gurukul) system to the pure bond between a guru(teacher) and a shishya (student). Our potential listeners and practitioners are from every age group. We want each person to experience this phenomenal art as it is an all-inclusive art form. Swaragini enlightens the audience about Indian Classical Music and its benefits, WORLDWIDE!

AUTUM DAWN

Music is not only about grooving over the beats, it’s also about letting the world know about your capabilities, strengths and may be beyond that. It’s an art that makes millions mesmerize to the core. Producing your own music, your own albums, having your own studio, giving live performances sounds cool but it’s indeed a very BIG task! Imagining of being a pop star fantasizes each one of us but it’s a duty with immense pressure and an unpredictable future.
