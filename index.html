ABOUT US

Imagine living in a world that is free from Melodies! Got Goosebumps! No one will love to go on a road trip with friends without playing ear-damaging loud songs! No one will love to attend festivals and ceremonies being celebrated without mesmerizing beats. Music bonds us together. It plays the role of a strong social adhesive. There is something about music that seems to bring us closer to each other and help us come together as a community. Listening to music and singing along with it directly impacts the chemicals in the brain, helping to develop a sense of companionship. Timbre Bells is all about celebrating music together, connecting people across the world through music, and giving a unified platform to all genres around the world. Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam- foundation of this project based on this mission of the next level of Globalization. We wish through our platform we connect all cultures existing on our planet in one knot. There is no such platform existing in this world which is connecting an infinite number of cultures and make us all stand like a BIG WORLD FAMILY! We desire to overcome the language barriers through music and make it available to all no matter who or where. TimbreBells strongly believes in लोकाःसमस्ताःसुखिनोभवन्तु, we aim at promoting our major ambition of “WORLD HAPPINESS” through music. The grounds of projects under Timbre Bells are about letting anyone and everyone put their music. Be it housewives singing and producing beats with their everyday household items to a street singer hypnotizing the crowd with rhythms. Be it a worldwide well-known artist or an amateur singer. Be it a 4-year-old kid or 60-year-old granny. We welcome everyone to come forward and fight against all odds to have a marvelous effect on the world!
