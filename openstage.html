How music helps me to deal with exam stress-
Being an engineering student is seriously a big task. Lots of assignments, practical, projects and what not! It’s a journey filled with fun but at the same time it’s challenging. The most tough part of engineering life is cracking exams. Exams – calling it as a package of stress. During the exams we require utmost focus on studies, we need to remain healthy and have proper sleep in order to maintain study schedule. Even after following proper schedule, many of us are not able to deal with exam pressure, extremely worried about results and how it will impact your career. I am one of the victims of this exam pressure, but Music is my savior which helps me to calm my mind. Usually after studying for several hours, I am always drowned in thoughts that whether I am going to do good or not.
